Kazakh Eli

Located in the center of the capital on Independence Square, Kazakh Eli Monument is a fairly new architectural landmark of Nur-Sultan. It is a column, standing on a quadrangular white marble foundation, which is decorated with bas-reliefs depicting the main events in the history of the independent Republic of Kazakhstan.
