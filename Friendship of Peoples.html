Friendship of Peoples
The monument, personifying the friendship and consent of the people of Kazakhstan, is made of bronze. It is a sculptural composition of three intersecting figures eight meters high. The figures that form the basis of the monument are expressive, as if the branches symbolize interlacing, shaking hands, peace and harmony.
